School Programs

Before & After School Programs

Safe, supervised care before and after the school day, plus enrichment classes through our After School Program.

🌅 Morning Stars

Hours: 7:00am – 8:30am (students must arrive by 8:00am)

Not available on: Delayed opening days

Snacks: Morning Stars students receive the free school breakfast when the school day begins.

🌇 CASPER After-Care

Hours: 2:45pm – 6:00pm

Not available on: Half days, teacher-parent conferences, or professional development days

Snacks: CASPER provides afternoon snacks.

Need financial assistance? Contact the Urban League at (201) 451-8888 or visit ulohc.org/programs/nj-cares-for-kids.
Payment policy: Monthly payments apply with a 5-day grace period. Children cannot attend if their balance is unpaid by the 10th of the month.

After School Enrichment Program (ASP)

The PS5 ASP offers a wide range of enrichment classes at reduced cost, with scholarships available.

Who: PreK 4 through 8th grade (not PreK 3)

When: Weekdays, 3:00pm – 5:00pm

Cost: Reduced fee; scholarships available — contact the PTA for details.

Current classes include:

  • Art
  • Breakdancing
  • Chess
  • Coding
  • Crochet
  • Dance
  • Beginner French
  • Lego
  • Mandarin
  • Musical Theatre
  • Soccer
  • Ukulele

Questions about the ASP? Email jerseycityps5pta@gmail.com.

PS5 PTA

The Jersey City PS5 PTA is a not-for-profit local affiliate of the National PTA.

Every donation we receive goes toward providing better and more services to our school so we can see all the children at PS5 reach their potential and beyond.
